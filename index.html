12 Hong Kong Fashionistas to Follow on Instagram

Isabel Marant
In fashion conscious, tech savvy Hong Kong, a handful of fashionistas stand out from the crowd. Their inspirational looks, beauty tips and travel shots might look effortless, but it takes a rare combination of style and effort for your Instagram game to be this strong. With that said, here are the top Hong Kong-based fashion Instagram accounts you should be following if you arent already.

Kaiti Yoo @kaitiyoo

fixing my identity crisis with a thicc YESSTYLE HAUL (GIVEAWAY!) Cindiddy.

Darrylinooo @darrylfastlife

A former fashion model, Darryl was born in Shanghai, raised in London and now lives in Hong Kong. He writes for fashion-oriented web magazines such as Jing Daily and Elle China, as well as his own blog.

Mayo Wo @mellowmayo

Mayo Wo, a travel and fashion blogger, admits to having an obsession with pastel hues. Her romantic Instagram snaps show off her modern and feminine style.

Ceci N @no7

Ceci Ns fashion style can be described as Hipster meets Goth. Shes the enigmatic founder of the Chinese language fashion and lifestyle app SENSE7.

Vincci Yang @vincci_yang

Vincci Yang, a Beijing native currently based in Hong Kong, is a fashion stylist, columnist and blogger.

Faye Tsui @faye_tsui

The effortlessly chic Faye Tsui is a well known fashion stylist and columnist. She also runs her own fashion blog, Fayevorite.

Samantha Wong @samishome

Samantha Wong is a graduate of New Yorks Parsons School of Design. Her stunningly curated Instagram is equally a source of inspiration and envy.

Visala @ vforvisala

With her vivid purple hair and keen eye for emerging labels, Visala is one of Hong Kongs hottest fashion bloggers.

Veronica Li @vnikali

Veronica Li is a fashion stylist and consultant with a background in design. She blogs about style, beauty and product reviews.

Geneva Vanderzeil @apairandaspare

Australian born Geneva Vanderzeil is no ordinary fashion blogger. Her incredibly successful website A Pair & A Spare, which focuses on DIY, fashion and lifestyle, is estimated to draw over 190,000 monthly visitors and is especially popular in the U.S.

Daisy Toi @daisytoi

23 year old Daisy Toi, who is of mixed Mongolian and Chinese descent, was crowned Miss Hong Kong 2016. Currently, shes a student at Hong Kong University.

Zoe Chan @zoe_normalchic

Zoe Chan is a stylist for some of Hong Kongs biggest newspapers and fashion magazines, including Sing Tao and JESSICA. She also runs her own fashion and lifestyle website, Normalchic.
